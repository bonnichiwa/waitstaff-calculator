Welcome to the Waitstaff Calculator. This convenient tool allows users to easily and quickly manage their earnings for their current shift. Simply enter in the meal details in the New Meal tab. The calculator will automatically calculate the subtotal, tip and total amount. Remember to only enter valid numbers! Once done, press submit to enter this into Your Earnings for this shift. At the end of your shift you can see your total tips amount, how many meals served and average tip per meal. Hitting the reset button will prepare you for your next shift with a blank form.
